Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions

1. Information We Collect

TarotApp only collects information you voluntarily provide during the setup process: your name (optional), date of birth, zodiac sign, and usage preferences. We do not collect contact information, email addresses, or social media data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your date of birth to calculate your sun sign, moon sign, rising sign, and Chinese zodiac sign. This information is used exclusively to personalize your experience within the app. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

All your personal information (name, date of birth) is stored encrypted on your device using iOS Keychain and Android EncryptedSharedPreferences. Your tarot reading history is saved locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

TarotApp may use the following third-party services: • RevenueCat: To manage subscriptions. Their privacy policy applies to purchase data. • Apple App Store / Google Play: To process payments. Their terms and conditions apply. These services have their own independent privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to: • Access all your data stored in the app • Correct any incorrect information • Permanently delete all your data (Settings → Delete my data) • Export your reading history

6. Children's Privacy

TarotApp is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
